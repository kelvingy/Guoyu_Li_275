British Shorthair

British shorthair cats have a chubby body, well-developed limbs, short and dense hair, a large head and a round face, gentle and calm, friendly to people, and easy to raise. Large, round eyes show various colors according to different coats. As an ancient cat breed, its history can be traced back to the domestic cats of ancient Rome.
